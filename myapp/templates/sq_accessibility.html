Q11. What accessibility issues are most reported (mobility, stroller, visual, audio)?

Why

To ensure compliance with accessibility standards and advance inclusive tourism goals for all visitors.

How

  • Apply keyword or LLM-based tagging for terms like “wheelchair”, “lift”, “stairs”, “stroller”, “ramp”.

Outputs

Q12. Are there safety, cleanliness, or maintenance concerns?

Why

To maintain public trust, protect reputation, and ensure operational excellence in all visitor environments.

How

  • Extract topics and keywords related to “theft”, “dirty”, “broken”, “dangerous”, “unclean”.
  • Perform severity scoring based on sentiment polarity and frequency of mentions.
  • Identify temporal or spatial clusters of maintenance or safety incidents.

Outputs

  • Incident trend dashboard with severity levels over time.
  • Attraction-level cleanliness and safety report cards.
  • Automated escalation alerts for recurrent or severe complaints.
