Q8. How do first-time vs. repeat visitors differ in needs and sentiment?

Why

To improve product-market fit and tailor the experience for both newcomers and loyal visitors.

How

  • Detect self-reported status in text (e.g., “first time”, “came again”, “return visit”).
  • Cluster reviews by behavioral patterns or visit frequency indicators.
  • Compare satisfaction, sentiment, and topic emphasis between both groups.

Outputs

  • Journey comparison charts between first-time and repeat visitors.
  • Segmented sentiment bars or spider charts showing emotional tone differences.
  • Targeted messaging strategies to strengthen loyalty and first impressions.
