Q16. What nearby amenities (food, restrooms, shade) need attention?

Why

To ensure onsite comfort, improve dwell time, and enhance overall visitor experience.

How

  • Analyze co-mentions of amenities like food, restrooms, and shaded areas in reviews.
  • Conduct sentiment analysis to detect dissatisfaction or gaps.
  • Identify areas lacking sufficient amenities.

Outputs

  • List of amenity gaps per site or attraction.
  • Recommendations for adding/restocking amenities.
  • Prioritization based on visitor impact and sentiment severity.
